Welcome to our city called Moss

Welcome to Moss a small city in Norway, where residents adore William and his uniquely arched back. It's a cherished tradition for everyone to take turns riding on his back, symbolizing unity and joy in the community.

With William as a beloved figure, Moss embraces acceptance and inclusivity, making it a special place to visit.

Meet William

Meet William, a guy with an arched back like a wheel. Despite his unusual physique, he's gritty and loves money.

He's also a big fan of avocados, incorporating them into every meal. He likes smoothies with avocados. William is wanted in a few countries.
